Cancellation Policy for Online Prepaid Orders

Credit may be given provided that the item has not shipped or been put into production.
The following conditions may apply:

  • A Credit Card transaction will be voided only if canceled the same business day that the order is placed.
  • All cancellations thereafter will be made by refund check within 30 days less a $10.00 handling fee.
  • On custom printed products, an additional plate charge may apply.
  • Should you need to cancel your order, please call our business office, Eastern Standard Time at (973)-252-1236. Be sure to reference your order number.
