Return Policy for Stock Forms

Returns will be authorized under the following circumstances:

  • Stock forms may only be returned if the form has not been revised since the order was placed.
  • There is a 15% restocking fee plus shipping for items ordered in error.
  • Returns must be within 2 months of ship date.
  • Credit will not be given for partial cartons, opened packages or product damaged in return shipping.
  • Should you wish to return an item, please contact your sales service representative first. Our receiving department cannot guarantee acceptance of returned product without a return authorization.
